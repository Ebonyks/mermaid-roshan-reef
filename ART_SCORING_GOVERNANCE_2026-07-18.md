# Art Scoring Governance Amendment — 2026-07-18 (Owner Decision)

Supersedes the "book = automatic 5/5" clause used by
`ART_HUMAN_REVIEW_AUDIT_2026-07-16.md` and earlier audits. Those documents
remain unmodified as historical records; audits dated after 2026-07-18 use
this definition.

## Why

The engine and pipeline changed. The game is a 3D cel-shaded diorama whose
world art is generated by deterministic Blender code and judged under real
lighting on the Mobile renderer at gameplay distances — conditions the static
book paintings were never designed for. With code-generated art, iteration is
nearly free (a generator edit plus a CI capture run), so the look can be
stress-tested toward a result that renders better in engine than a book-
fidelity cap would allow. Book fidelity as a *quality ceiling* is therefore
retired; book art as *identity* is not.

## The scale after this amendment

- **5/5** — the asset survived the full stress-test loop: representative
  near, mid, and gameplay-distance Mobile-renderer captures, with visual
  rejection iterations until clean, and explicit owner acceptance of the
  shipped views. Applies uniformly to every asset, including book-derived
  ones. (The Northern Kingdom rebuild of 2026-07-17 is the reference example
  of the loop: two candidate iterations rejected on visual review before
  acceptance.)
- **4/5 and below** — unchanged from the 2026-07-16 rubric, including the
  hard caps (no runtime captures → cap 2; primitive focal object → cap 1;
  and the rest).
- There is no longer any automatic score for provenance. The 52+ roles
  recorded as `4_candidate_owner_review` now have a reachable ceiling: pass
  the loop, get accepted, score 5.

## Two pipelines, one review gate

| | World / environment assets | Book-derived characters & story objects |
|---|---|---|
| Workflow | Deterministic Blender generation (code), iterated through the capture loop | Meshy image-to-3D from the book art (nano-banana-based), preserving the source's painted modeling quality by construction |
| Source of quality | Stress-test iteration | Source fidelity of the book painting |
| Review gate | Same for both: Mobile captures at near/mid/gameplay distance + owner acceptance | Same |

Meshy-derived assets from book art are sanctioned. The untouched originals in
`assets/book/`, `assets/audio/voices/`, and `assets/characters/friends/`
remain protected exactly as before — never destroyed, overwritten, or
destructively recompressed — and stay the identity reference (Roshan's
anchors: chestnut hair, front-left rainbow forelock, lavender clothing,
green-right/pink-left tail).

## What this does not change

- Content protection of originals (CLAUDE.md hard rule).
- The no-fail-state, non-reader, one-finger design constraints.
- The Mobile-renderer-everywhere rule and asset budget rules.
- The style guide's shape/line/value/color language — the stress-test loop
  tests execution of that language, it does not replace it.
