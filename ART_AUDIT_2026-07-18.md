# Art Audit — 2026-07-18 (Four-Day Window: 2026-07-14 → 2026-07-18)

Repeat audit covering all art added or replaced in the past four days, rated on
the runtime gameplay-gate scale defined in `ART_HUMAN_REVIEW_AUDIT_2026-07-16.md`
(0 = broken/debug … 5 = book-source or validated-exceptional; a generated
candidate cannot exceed 4 without owner screenshot review).

Evidence used: git history for the window, GLB/texture parsing of every new
file (vertex/triangle/material/palette statistics), reference-mapping of every
asset into `scripts/` and `scenes/`, `ASSET_LICENSES.md` coverage, the
pass-3.5 Mobile capture audit, the 2026-07-17 placement and Northern-world
audits, and CI probe results (green through the window's final master state).
No new Mobile screenshots were captured for this audit, so no rating here is
promoted past `4_candidate_owner_review`.

## Technical profile — new art vs. the old library

Comparison sample: 36 pre-window GLBs from `assets/{aquatic,kits,galaxy,castle,props/gen2}`.
Parsed set: the 139 window GLBs through commit `935fc79`; the 17-piece Northern
rebuild (`assets/northern/`, commit `917a39e`) landed via a parallel branch and
is profiled from its own audit's numbers in the Northern section below.

| Metric | Old library (n=36) | Window additions (n=139) |
|---|---|---|
| Mean vertices | 3,355 (median 1,366) | 1,767 (median 1,125) |
| Mean materials per model | 1.6 | 4.9 |
| Distinct base colours set-wide | 26 | 144 |
| Pure-white material slots | 53% | 3% |
| Embedded textures | 67% of files | 6% (fairy relief models only) |
| Mean file size | 0.35 MB | 0.115 MB |
| Trivial primitives (<100 verts, 1 flat material) | present (e.g. `aquatic/Rock.glb`, 84 v) | **zero** |

The structural shift is exactly what the style guide asked for: the window's
models are texture-free, multi-material flat-colour builds whose palette lives
in `baseColorFactor` slots, sharing one deliberate pastel set
(coral `#ffa399`, lavender `#a87dd6`, gold `#f5b838`, aqua `#45c4c7`,
mint `#80d48f`, cream `#f5ebd1`) plus navy/indigo outline-shell materials
(`#4a4f78`, `#1a1238`, `#090d1a`). Geometry is roughly half the vertices and a
third the bytes of the old library while carrying three times the colour
information. Every window texture obeys the ≤1024-or-POT rule.

## Findings & actions

1. **Orphaned deliverables (11):** never placed —
   `art35/arena/fairy_bank_0.glb`, `fairy_bank_1.glb`,
   `fairy_lily_cluster.glb`, `fairy_shadow_beetle.glb`,
   `art35/kart/soft_barrier.glb`; superseded same-day by the Northern rebuild —
   all six `art35/northern/*.glb`. Place or delete the first five; delete the
   northern six. Today they cost import time and license rows for zero
   on-screen value.
2. **Byproduct textures shipping in the repo (~150, mostly from the 07-13
   rescue):** loose `_Image_0`/colormap/nano extractions duplicate texture data
   already embedded in their GLBs and will be swept into the APK export.
   Candidate for deletion or export-exclude; largest offenders are
   `galaxy/butterfly1_butterflyTxt.png` (3.1 MB) and
   `galaxy/trop_palm2_*BaseColor.png` (2.2 MB).
3. **Texture-rule violations: none in the window.** Two pre-window files from
   the 07-13 rescue violate ≤1024-or-POT: `characters/roshan_0.png`
   (1610×2048) and `characters/huluu_huluu.png` (640×1039). Character cutouts,
   so likely intentional — but they should be either resized/POT-padded or
   documented as exceptions.
4. **Un-merged export:** `castle/bathroom_sink.glb` (18 materials,
   21 primitives). Merge by material in the generator.
5. **Pipeline consistency:** fairy boss models are the only textured GLBs in
   the new corpus; consider migrating them to the flat-colour material
   pipeline for style unity.
6. **Licensing: compliant.** All window additions are covered by grouped
   project-generated rows in `ASSET_LICENSES.md` with generator provenance
   (`build_art_pass35.py`, `build_reef_region_kit.py`,
   `build_collection_critters.py`, `build_dungeon_art_v2.py`, kitchen/bathroom
   builders, fairy relief pipeline, alpine chalet builder, low-score batches).
7. **Validation status:** trusted probes green on CI through the window's
   final master state, including the placement-continuity assertions and the
   Northern rebuild's four capture-validated Actions runs. Zero regressions to
   0–1/5; nothing shipped in the window rates below 3. The remaining gate is
   unchanged from pass 3.5: owner screenshot review to promote the
   4-candidates, plus the untouched residuals (reef hub exposure, castle
   halls, kart world composition, lagoon terrain).

## Verdict

The four days delivered 249 art items with no style regressions and a clear
technical break from the primitive/legacy era: median model is lighter, palette
is unified pastel-with-indigo-outlines, no trivial primitives, and every area's
"before" complaints from the 2026-07-16 human audit were answered somewhere in
the window except castle halls, kart world composition and lagoon terrain —
which are now the three oldest open items on the board. The Northern Kingdom
set the window's evidence bar: generate, capture on the Mobile renderer,
reject twice, and only then accept — the same loop the 52+ remaining
4-candidates still need owner review to close. Weighted by role count, the
window moves the audited median from 1–2/5 to 3.5.
