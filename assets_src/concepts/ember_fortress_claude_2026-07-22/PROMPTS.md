## 01_world_layout.png

Design the complete spherical volcanic play-world Ember Fortress: authored
planet shell, two broad lava rivers, north polar citadel plaza, south polar
home portal plaza, readable basalt landmasses, storm sky enclosure, and sparks.
Show a large three-quarter planet plus north-pole, south-pole, and side views.
Use approximately 80 m planet diameter, 20 m north plaza, and 15 m south plaza.
Avoid characters, franchise imagery, horror, crushed blacks, and surface noise.

## 02_citadel_architecture.png

length 5.6 m, flag height 2.4 m, gate height 9 m, and veil height 6.2 m. The
